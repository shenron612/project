Data Protection

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 2026

01. Information We Collect

To provide a functional marketplace, HireLink collects the following information:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and encrypted password.
  • Professional Data: Skills, hourly rates, and working availability.
  • Transaction Data: Phone numbers used for M-Pesa payments and transaction reference IDs.

02. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for platform operations, including:

  • Matching workers with relevant job opportunities.
  • Processing secure payments via the Safaricom Daraja API.
  • Providing support and dispute resolution through HireLink Care.
  • Improving platform security and preventing fraudulent activity.

03. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data sharing is limited to:

  • Employers/Workers: Sharing contact info once a booking is confirmed.
  • Safaricom: Sending payment requests to the M-Pesa gateway.

We implement standard SSL encryption and hashed password storage to protect your information.

04. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. You can manage your profile visibility and professional details through your User Dashboard.

05. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your M-Pesa data is handled, please contact our support team at privacy@hirelink.co.ke.
