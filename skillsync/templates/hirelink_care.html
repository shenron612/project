Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you need to know about HireLink Care.

Who can file a dispute? ›
Anyone registered on HireLink — both workers and employers — can file a dispute at any time. You don't need to be the paying party to report an issue.
How long does resolution take? ›
Most disputes are resolved within 24 hours. Complex cases involving fraud or large sums may take up to 72 hours. You'll receive updates throughout the process.
What evidence should I provide? ›
Screenshots of conversations, payment receipts, job agreements, photos of incomplete work, and any other relevant documentation all help speed up resolution.
Can I appeal a decision? ›
Yes. If you believe the outcome was unfair, you can request a re-review within 48 hours of the decision by replying to your resolution email with new evidence.
Is HireLink Care free? ›
Absolutely. HireLink Care is a free service available to all registered users. There are no fees to file a dispute or request mediation.
